What Are Databases Used For?

  1. Streaming

    All streaming platforms such as Netflix or Amazon use databases to provide you with content relevant to movies you have already watched.

  2. Gaming

    Popular video games require databases to function without significant bottleneck or lag. They are especially useful during times where many people are playing at the same time.

  3. Cloud Storage

    Syncing your photos across all of your devices requires the service provider to have powerful databases so that they can call upon your data at a moments notice.

  4. Banks

    All banks use databases to keep track of your transations. They also use them to keep track of the stock market and to provide you with future predictions.
